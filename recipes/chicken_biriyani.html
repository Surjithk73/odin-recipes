Chicken Biriyani Recipe

DESCRIPTION

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are also added.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato Puree
  • Fried Onions
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Basmati Rice
  • Oil & Ghee

STEPS

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Marinate the chicken for 1 hour
  3. Add fried onions, chopped tomatoes & green chillies and fry
  4. Add the marinated chicken
  5. Layer cooked chicken with partially cooked rice, sprinkle with chopped coriander and mint leaves
  6. Drizzle ghee on top, cover pot with a tight lid, cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
